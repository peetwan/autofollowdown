# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in
autofollowdown a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and welcoming to newcomers.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
personal or political attacks, and publishing others' private information without
consent.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the
project maintainers via a GitHub issue or direct contact. Maintainers will review
and respond to reports, and may remove, edit, or reject contributions or comments
that violate this Code of Conduct.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
